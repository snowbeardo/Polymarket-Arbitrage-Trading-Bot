## Polymarket BTC Momentum Trading Bot

A Python-based automated trading bot for Polymarket crypto Up/Down markets that leverages Bitcoin momentum and cross-market confirmation signals from Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and XRP.

The core idea behind this project is simple:

Bitcoin often acts as the leading indicator for the broader cryptocurrency market. When BTC develops strong directional momentum, major altcoins frequently follow the same direction. This behavior is especially noticeable in short-duration Polymarket crypto markets as they approach settlement.

This bot continuously monitors the order books of BTC, ETH, SOL, and XRP Up/Down markets in real time using WebSocket streams and market APIs. When Bitcoin reaches a high-confidence probability threshold and one or more correlated markets confirm the same direction, the bot automatically executes trades, holds positions until market resolution, and redeems winning shares.

The trading logic focuses on identifying high-conviction momentum signals near market expiration.

### Why This Bot?

Most prediction market strategies focus on complex indicators, statistical models, or heavy risk management systems.

This project explores a different approach:

Rather than predicting market direction from scratch, it attempts to identify moments when multiple crypto markets are already showing strong directional agreement and automate execution around those signals.

The goal is to build a lightweight, event-driven trading system capable of reacting to market momentum in real time.
